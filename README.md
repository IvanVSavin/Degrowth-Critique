# Degrowth-Critique
Files associated with STM analysis of 561 studies on degrowth and postgrowth, and published in "Reviewing studies of degrowth: Are claims matched by data, methods and policy analysis?" 
https://doi.org/10.1016/j.ecolecon.2024.108324

The folder contains the details on 561 studies (final sample degrowth review.csv) and all the files needed to reproduce STM results  regarding topics covered, journals where the studies are published, heatmaps of countries with author affiliations etc.
